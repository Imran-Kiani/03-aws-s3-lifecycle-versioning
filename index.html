Data Resilience & Disaster Recovery (S3 Versioning)

вЂЋВ В В В В В В В В В В 

To ensure high availability and protect the production environment against accidental deletions or malicious overwrites, I enabled Amazon S3 Bucket Versioning via Terraform.

вЂЋВ В В В В В В В В В В 

Every time a new artifact or deployment is pushed through the CI/CD pipeline, S3 automatically keeps track of older iterations. This guarantees a robust Rollback StrategyвЂ”in the event of a faulty deployment, the website can be restored to a previous stable state within seconds, minimizing downtime and maintaining business continuity.
